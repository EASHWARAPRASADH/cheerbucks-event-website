Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and 1–2 months for smaller gatherings. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What areas do you cover? +

We primarily serve Chennai and surrounding areas, but we also manage events across Tamil Nadu and South India upon request.

Do you offer customized packages? +

Absolutely! Every event is unique, and we tailor our services to match your vision, budget, and requirements. Contact us for a custom quote.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellation terms vary by event type and size. We discuss these details clearly during the initial consultation and include them in our service agreement.
